About

Virtual Health enables individuals to access to a doctor, a specialist, or a nurse practitioner.
Patients can book an appointment with a doctor depending on their condition, saving them
time and stress of having to wait for long queues in the emergency departments.
Still, patients can consult doctors via the virtual consultation feature 24/7.
